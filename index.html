Let us HELP you!!

'TechAssist' was developed to help people who are not up to date with technology. We discovered that there is a huge number of people who are not up to date with technology, so to minimize that count we developed 'TechAssist' and we provide free services to those who need our help.

We would like to assist you with your technological purchases. Our objective is to help you to get the best products for the best prices.

We also conduct technical sessions where you can get answers to all your technological problems. To make these sessions more effective we conduct them as a personal consultation service.

Our main objective is to help people and make a positive impact in the community.
